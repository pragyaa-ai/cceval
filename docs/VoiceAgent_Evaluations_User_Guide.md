## Introduction

**VoiceAgent Evaluations** is an advanced HR services evaluation platform designed to assess and improve communication skills for HR professionals. The platform leverages sophisticated VoiceAgent technology to provide real-time voice analysis, intelligent conversation evaluation, and comprehensive performance metrics.

### Key Features

- **Real-time Voice Analysis**: Assess pitch, volume, clarity, and pace during live conversations
- **Intelligent Conversation Evaluation**: Evaluate soft skills, probing abilities, and communication effectiveness
- **Use Case Specific Metrics**: Tailored evaluation criteria for Exit Interviews, New Hire Engagement (NHE), and Continuous Engagement (CE)
- **Gamified Learning**: Continuous improvement through practice sessions with rewards
- **Comprehensive Analytics**: Detailed insights and trend analysis for performance improvement

### Platform Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Evaluator** | HR managers and trainers who manage candidates, review results, and configure evaluation criteria |
| **Candidate** | Employees or trainees undergoing evaluation for their communication skills |
| **Continuous Learner** | Employees engaging in self-paced learning and practice to improve skills |

### Feedback & Calibration

The Feedback system allows evaluators to refine and improve evaluation accuracy.

#### Providing Feedback on Results

1. Open a completed evaluation from the Results tab
2. Click **"Provide Feedback"** button
3. For each metric score:
   - Review the VoiceAgent-assigned score
   - Compare with your professional assessment
   - Adjust the score if needed (1-10 scale)
   - Add comments explaining your adjustment

4. Click **"Submit Feedback"** to save

#### Understanding Calibration

Calibration helps align VoiceAgent assessments with your organization's standards:

1. **Initial Assessment**: VoiceAgent provides automated scoring
2. **Expert Review**: Evaluators review and adjust scores
3. **Feedback Loop**: Your adjustments help refine future evaluations
4. **Continuous Improvement**: The system learns from calibration data

#### Recalibration Process

When you need to recalibrate scores:

1. Navigate to the **Feedback** tab
2. Select evaluations requiring recalibration
3. Click **"Recalibrate"**
4. The VoiceAgent backend will reprocess the evaluation with updated parameters
5. Compare new scores with previous scores
6. Approve or request further adjustments

#### Calibration Best Practices

- Calibrate regularly (recommended: weekly)
- Involve multiple evaluators for consistency
- Document reasoning for significant score adjustments
- Review calibration trends monthly

### Evaluation Process

Your evaluation consists of three main phases:

#### Phase 1: System Check

- âœ… Microphone access and quality

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: VoiceAgent Evaluations works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Ensure your browser is up to date.

**Q: Why do I need microphone access?**
A: Voice evaluations require microphone access to analyze your speech. This is essential for accurate assessment.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorized evaluators can access your results.

**Q: Can I retake an evaluation?**
A: Formal evaluations can only be retaken if authorized by an evaluator. Practice sessions can be repeated unlimited times.

### For Evaluators

**Q: How do I add candidates in bulk?**
A: Use the Bulk Upload feature in Candidate Management. Download the CSV template, fill in candidate details, and upload.

**Q: Can I customize evaluation metrics?**
A: Yes, go to Scenarios > Scoring Metrics to add, edit, or remove metrics for each use case.

**Q: How often should I calibrate?**
A: We recommend reviewing and calibrating scores weekly to maintain accuracy.

**Q: Can multiple evaluators manage the same candidates?**
A: Yes, all evaluators with access can manage candidates and view results.

### For Candidates

**Q: What if my microphone isn't working?**
A: Check your browser permissions, ensure the correct microphone is selected, and try refreshing the page.

**Q: Can I pause during an evaluation?**
A: Evaluations are designed to be completed in one sitting. If you experience technical issues, contact your evaluator.

**Q: How long does an evaluation take?**
A: Typically 10-15 minutes, including the reading passage and role-play scenario.

**Q: When will I see my results?**
A: Results are available immediately after completing your evaluation.

### For Continuous Learners

**Q: Do practice sessions count toward my formal evaluation?**
A: No, practice sessions are for learning only and don't affect formal evaluation scores.

**Q: How do I earn points faster?**
A: Complete daily challenges, maintain practice streaks, and aim for high scores.

**Q: Can I lose points?**
A: Points can only be spent on rewards; you cannot lose earned points through inactivity.

**Q: When do rewards expire?**
A: Check individual reward details; some may have expiration dates set by your organization.
